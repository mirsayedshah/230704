B03(t+1) [kWh]

This simulation is designed to predict the future values of the system's most critical bus (B03), taking into account the influence of the only most impactful buses on B03.

The impact of these buses on the critical bus is considered under two conditions: their value in a previous time period (t-1) and their steady-state value before the simulation.

The term 'buses at the (t) timeframe' refers to the current value of buses during the simulation, while the timeframe of (t+1) indicates a future or forecasted load value for a bus.

Identifying critical buses through sensitivity analysis enables efficient resource allocation, facilitates the integration of renewable energy sources by managing variability, and

maintains system stability by pinpointing potential vulnerabilities to voltage or frequency fluctuations.
